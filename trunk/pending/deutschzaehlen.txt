 The UND appears only if there is numberUNDtennernumber; if either is absent, so is UND.

 The gender can be made clear in two grammatical constructions
       erster Mann or der erste Mann     (meaning the first man)
       zweite Frau or die zweite Frau    (meaning the second woman}
       drittes Kind or das dritte Kind   (meaning the third child)
       This however does not work with ein/eine/ein (meaning "a" rather than "the")
       Genders for day and month are "male": der Tag, der Monat
                   for year it is "neutral": das Jahr

 Final example: todays date
     vierundzwanzigster Tag des vierten Monats April im Jahr zweitausendsieben
     twentyfourth       day of the fourth month Aprin in the year twothousandseven.
